Hard days at school

Katelyn's Homepage

Once opon a time there was a girl named Sarah. She didn't want to go to school today. She didn't want to deal with all of
her classmates that always kept teasing her. "Mom, I don't want to go to school today." Sarah called. "Why? Its such a nice day?"
Mom asked."My classmates have been teasing me lately because they are jealous about me being the top of the class." Sarah answered.
"Well, you better get to school. Its starting soon." Her mom warned her. Sarah headed out the door and called goodbye to her mother.
She braced herself for the loud, noisy bus that she was walking into. Just as Sarah had predicted the bus was as loud as usual.
Sarah dreaded the long walk from the school entrence to her classroom. Her classroom was at the very back of the school.
